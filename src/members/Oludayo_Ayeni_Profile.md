## Summary

---

> An alumnus of Lagos State University and the University of Lagos where he obtained his undergraduate and post-graduate degrees respectively. He has had the rare privilege of working with several senior advocates of Nigeria.

> He is an Attorney with experience in corporate commercial and dispute resolution transactions. His vast experience and versatility as an Attorney span diverse areas of the law which includes dispute resolution and corporate commercial practices. He was a part of various legal teams both locally and internationally, that have managed a variety of sensitive legal briefs and he has represented the interests of various clients as well as agencies of the Nigerian government.

> Oludayo has participated in various court proceedings, and he is familiar with the Nigerian court system and processes. He has an excellent work ethic and always prioritizes the best interests of clients while maintaining the highest professional standards.

<summary>Track Record</summary>

---

- Part of the team of lawyers that advised the various Houses of Assembly in the southwest region of Nigeria on recommendations for the 4th Alteration to the 1999 Constitution of the Federal Republic of Nigeria.

- Advised and formulated strategies in the representation of a foreign client in court in a case of tortious interference occasioning a breach of contract. Coordinated information and investigation of lost assets (rare metals such as cassiterite and columbite) in Nigeria and provided information which culminated in the payment of $10,000,000 (Ten Million United States Dollars) in insurance claims to the client. Also handled general debt recovery briefs for various clients.

- Pivotal member of a team of lawyers that upturned a death sentence of an Appellant at the Court of Appeal. Also secured the discharge and acquittal of a Defendant who was charged with the offense of armed robbery at the High Court.

- Represented the interests of some Federal Government Agencies in the Nigerian energy sector in court as it relates to their acquisition of landed properties for the development of various energy projects in unserved communities and defending claims arising from the terms of the contract in the implementation of the project.

- Managed over N6,000,000,000 (Six Billion Naira) in various property portfolios for private clients, banks, and local and international companies, i.e., mortgages, tenancies, and leases, negotiated the sale and acquisition of properties, carried out due diligence checks on the properties at the relevant land registries prior to the disbursement of loans or purchase.
